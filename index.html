About

My name is Alexys Onyemakonor and I'm an Arkansas-native residing in Philadelphia, PA. I have over three years of experience in databases as a Senior Developer at a Merkle Inc, a global marketing agency. At Merkle, I am responsible for the development and production maintainence of several financial client databases. My days are tasked with writing scripts for the purpose of automating data loading and exporting, resolving production errors, and finding ways to improve exisiting solutions.
